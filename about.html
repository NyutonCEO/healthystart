About Healthy Start

Healthy Start Medical Transportation is a non‑emergency medical and ancillary transportation company delivering consistent, reliable on‑time service. We are dedicated to door‑to‑door service and provide mobility, safety, and care to seniors, disabled individuals, and veterans for medical and treatment appointments — as well as shopping, recreational, and social events.

Did You Know?

  • 3.6 million Americans miss or delay care each year due to lack of transportation.
  • Half of Americans 65+ do not have access to public transportation.
  • More than half of non‑drivers 65+ stay home on a given day for lack of transportation options.
