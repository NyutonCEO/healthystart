Frequently Asked Questions

About Our Services

What is Healthy Start Non‑Emergency Transportation?▾

We provide safe, reliable rides for seniors, veterans, and individuals with disabilities to get to medical appointments, therapy, pharmacies, and community activities.

What areas do you serve?▾

We currently serve multiple counties across Eastern and Central North Carolina, including Wilson, Halifax, Hertford, Nash, Johnston, Wake, Durham, Orange, and Edgecombe.

Do you only provide medical transportation?▾

No. In addition to medical appointments, we also provide rides for senior center outings, veterans’ activities, church services, and other approved trips.

Booking & Scheduling

How do I book a ride?▾

You can schedule online through our “Book My Ride” portal or call our call center at (252) 674‑1812.

How far in advance should I reserve my trip?▾

Please book at least 24 hours in advance. Same‑day rides are available by phone, depending on driver availability.

Can I schedule recurring rides?▾

Yes. We can set up standing reservations for dialysis, physical therapy, or other recurring appointments.

Costs & Payments

How much does a ride cost?▾

Rates vary based on distance, wheelchair access, and group trips. Local Wilson rides start at $20 roundtrip, and out‑of‑town rides are calculated at $2.50 per mile with a minimum fare. Pricing is subject to change.

Do you accept insurance or Medicaid?▾

At this time we do not accept Medicaid, Medicare, or private insurance.

What payment options are available?▾

We accept cash, debit/credit card, PayPal, and checks.

Drivers & Vehicles

Are your vehicles wheelchair accessible?▾

Yes. We offer cars, SUVs, vans, and buses — including wheelchair accessible vehicles.

What training do your drivers have?▾

All drivers undergo background checks, NC Health Registry clearance, and training in CPR, crisis response, defensive driving, and passenger assistance.

Can my caregiver or family member ride with me?▾

Absolutely. One caregiver, family member, or friend may ride with you free of charge if you share the same pickup and drop‑off location.

Service Hours & Availability

What are your hours of operation?▾

Transportation runs Monday–Saturday, 8:00am–11:59pm, with Sunday service available by reservation. Our call center is open Monday–Saturday, 7:00am–5:00pm.

Do you provide transportation on weekends or holidays?▾

Yes, we provide service evenings, weekends, and holidays.

Do you offer door‑to‑door service?▾

Yes. We pick you up at your front door and drop you at your destination entrance. Our drivers can assist with packages and personal items at no additional charge.

Have another question? Contact us — we’re happy to help.
