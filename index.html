How it works

1. Request

Patients, caregivers, or staff provide pickup, drop‑off, and appointment time.

2. Match

We assign the right vehicle (standard, wheelchair) and confirm ETA.

3. Ride

On‑time pickup and drop‑off with status updates. Billing routed per payer.

We are not affiliated with Lyft; this is an independent service tailored for NEMT.
