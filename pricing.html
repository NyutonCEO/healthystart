Pricing & Quotes

Clear, upfront rates for individuals and organizations. Call for exact quotes.

Standard Rates

  • Local (Wilson): Rides start at $20 roundtrip.
  • Out‑of‑town: $4.15 per mile with a minimum fare.
  • Wheelchair‑accessible: Available; rates vary based on trip details.
  • Group trips: Priced based on size and distance.
  • Payment: Cash, debit/credit card, PayPal, and checks.

Pricing is subject to change. Final quotes depend on distance, accessibility needs, and scheduling.

Group Shuttles & Notes

  • Wilson group shuttle: $5.00 per person (local events and outings)
  • Out‑of‑town group shuttle: $4.15 per mile + $5.00 per person
  • Minimum fares: One‑way $45.00; Round‑trip $85.00 (out‑of‑town)
  • Miles calculated: From pick‑up address to drop‑off address and return
  • Tolls & parking: Passenger responsibility when applicable

Discounts

  • 1 ride/week for a month: 5% discount
  • 3+ rides in a week: 10% discount
  • 10+ rides in a month: 15% discount
  • Discounts cannot be combined.

Insurance & Medicaid

At this time we do not accept Medicaid, Medicare, or private insurance. If you’re an organization or broker exploring contracted services, please contact our team.

What affects price?

  • Pickup and drop‑off distance
  • Wheelchair accessibility and assistance needs
  • Peak times, holidays, or special scheduling
